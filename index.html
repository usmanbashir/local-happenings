Local Happenings - Prototype Version: 1.0

First and for most this is a very fast & roughly built prototype so there are bound to be bugs & loose ends but I hope you can look past those and get a feel for the concept behind it.

There were so many features that I wanted to add but could not due to the lack of time and the need to get the prototype out fast.

But if you have any comments, suggestions or bug reports about this mash up then please feel free to email me at me@usmanbashir.com.

You can also follow me on Twitter at @ubax and for more information about me please visit usmanbashir.com.

Thanks for checking out this mash up.

Note: It seems that the detection of the users current geo location is only working properly in Firefox 3.6 and Google Chrome Canary Build 11 for the time being. I will look in to this issue in detail as soon as I can.
